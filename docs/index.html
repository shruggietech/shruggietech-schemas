ShruggieTech Schemas Collection

Welcome to ShruggieTech Schemas, a collection of JSON Schemas for validating data structures in various applications. Our schemas are designed to be easy to use and integrate into your projects, ensuring that your data is structured correctly and consistently.

Explore our collection of schemas below and feel free to contribute or suggest new ones!
